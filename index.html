The Process

How HydroGuard Works

1. Real-time Monitoring

State-of-the-art biological and chemical sensors continuously analyze village water sources for pathogens and pollutants.

2. Predictive Analysis

Cloud algorithms cross-reference clinical reports from health workers with sensor data to detect potential outbreaks before they spread.

3. Instant Response

Immediate alerts are sent to citizens and authorities via SMS and the HydroGuard app, mobilizing rapid localized treatment teams.
